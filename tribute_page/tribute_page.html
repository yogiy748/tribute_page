Sushant Singh Rajput

1986-2020

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor who made a remarkable impact in the film industry. Born on January 21, 1986, he showcased his exceptional talent and versatility throughout his career.

Starting his journey as a television actor, Sushant gained immense popularity for his role in the TV show "Pavitra Rishta." He then made a successful transition to the big screen, delivering memorable performances in films such as "Kai Po Che!," "MS Dhoni: The Untold Story," and "Chhichhore."

Known for his passion for science, astronomy, and technology, Sushant was not only an actor but also an avid learner and explorer. He actively promoted education and encouraged young minds to pursue their dreams.

Tragically, Sushant Singh Rajput passed away on June 14, 2020, leaving behind a void in the hearts of his fans and the film industry. His legacy as an exceptional actor and his positive influence on people's lives will always be remembered.
